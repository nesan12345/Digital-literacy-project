Stay Safe Online: A Guide for College Students 

Verify the Source: Never click on links or download attachments from unexpected emails or SMS messages, even if they appear to be from your bank or university. 

Check the URL: Before entering any login credentials, ensure the website address starts with https:// and the domain name is spelled correctly. 

Use Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Enable 2FA or MFA on all your primary accounts, including GitHub, LinkedIn, and your college email. 

Practice UPI Safety (Tip 1): Never enter your UPI PIN to RECEIVE money. A PIN is only required for sending money or checking your balance. 

Practice UPI Safety (Tip 2): Always verify the name of the recipient on the UPI app's confirmation screen before hitting 'Send.' 

Secure Your Hardware: Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions. Use your mobile data or a trusted home network instead. 

Update Regularly: Keep your smartphone operating system and banking apps updated to the latest versions. 

Limit Social Sharing: Avoid posting your phone number or home address on public social media profiles.
