Social Media Do's and Don'ts for Students

DO'S

* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Protect your accounts from unauthorized access by adding an extra security layer.
* Use Professional Profile Pictures: On platforms like LinkedIn, use a clear headshot to present a professional image to recruiters.
* Audit Your Privacy Settings: Regularly check who can see your personal posts and restrict access to trusted friends only.
* Verify Information: Always double-check facts or academic news from official sources before sharing or reposting.
* Network with Industry Professionals: Use social media to follow companies and professionals in the AIML field to stay updated on trends.

DON'TS

* Post Confidential Information: Never share internal college circulars, exam papers, or private student data.
* Engage in Cyberbullying: Avoid aggressive comments, trolling, or any behavior that could be seen as online harassment.
* Overshare Real-Time Location: For personal safety, avoid tagging your exact location while you are still there.
* Use Unprofessional Language: Avoid using offensive language, slang, or inappropriate symbols in your public bios or comments.
* Ignore Your Digital Footprint: Don't post anything that could negatively impact a background check by a future employer.
